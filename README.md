# Table tennis (single player)

This a game that is basicaly the online version of table tennis. This version is single player where you control one paddle and keep the ball in play as long as possible.

---

## How to Play

- Move your mouse up and down to control the paddle
- Hit the ball to keep it in play
- The ball bounces off the top, bottom, and right wall
- If you miss the ball, your score resets
